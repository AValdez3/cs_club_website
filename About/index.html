About the Computer Science Club

We are the Computer Science Club and we are dedicated to helping out our fellow Computer Science majors by providing various workshops to expose them to technologies varying in Web Development to Arduino Programming. Our mission is to simply get CS students coding and building cool projects that they can add to their resume and be proud to show them off! We will also be hosting the first ever Hackathon on campus to get CS students involved in Software Engineering and work as teams to win a prize! The club will offer so many things for the CS students, so please show your support and sign up today!
